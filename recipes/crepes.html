Description

Fast and easy to make ordinary crepes. Then, you can add anything you want!

Ingredients

  • 120g baking flour
  • 100ml of milk
  • 2 eggs
  • A pinch of sugar
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Mix

    2. Combine milk and eggs in a bowl. Then, mix together the resulting mixture and the flour by adding it little by little, avoiding lumps from appearing.

  2. Cook

    3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan medium high heat. Pour the batter onto the pan, using approximately quarter of a cup for each crepe.

    Cook until crepes are golden brown, 1 to 3 minutes per side.

  3. Serve

    4. Add your favourite toppings to the crepe, serve hot and enjoy.
